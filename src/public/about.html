This website is about chess ratings. The ratings are based on the ELO rating assigned to each player by FIDE

There are 427,952 players in the database, and data is available for each player and their ratings between 2016-2019

  • You can find a player's FIDE ID by searching their first name.
  • For each player, you can find their rating for a specific month, or aggregated by year.
  • You can input updated rating using the submit-chess link on the nav bar
  • Those ratings will be displayed under the speed layer
  • Finally, you can see the information also aggregated by federations
